c. AI Idea Reflection

Idea Chosen: AI-Powered Personalized Learning Apps

The idea that stood out most to me from the “AI Businesses of 2025” blog was AI-powered Personalized Learning. This technology adapts lessons, quizzes, and activities to each student’s learning style and speed. In my world, it could make studying easier by providing feedback on my weak areas and creating customized study plans. This would save time and help improve my academic performance.

However, ethical concerns include data privacy and AI bias, as systems might collect personal data or favor certain learning types. To address this, clear data policies and regular system checks should be in place.An example of this technology is Khan Academy, which uses AI tutoring through Khanmigo to offer personalized learning support.
